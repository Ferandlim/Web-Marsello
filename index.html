We have been serving our dishes since 1962

Restaurant Marcello's elements of traditional Italian cuisine come together with attention to detail and antique furniture, making it a pleasant and charming place to eat. Its dining room with a fireplace for cold winter evenings and a pergola with warm summer evenings is equally inviting.

The Marcello family has been running this restaurant for 60 years, with Jarvis in the kitchen and his wife Jane in the dining room.

Our restaurant's space

Restaurant Marcello's was founded in the 1962s and initially operated as a barn. After various managements, in 1990, it was taken over by its current owners and managers, Jarvis and Jane.

The premises are those of a farmhouse dating back to the 17th century and, still today, with a management strongly linked to Italian tradition; the tratorria offers picturesque corners created by exposed beams and a typical fireplace.
